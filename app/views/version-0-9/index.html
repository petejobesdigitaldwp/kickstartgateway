Apply for a Kickstart Scheme Grant for Employers

Use this service to apply for a Kickstart Scheme employer grant. You can use this service if you are an employer or represent a group of employers and have 30 or more Kickstart job placements.

Check if you can apply for a grant through the Kickstart Scheme.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Before you start

You will need:

  • the Companies House reference number or Charity Commission number
  • the organisation address and contact details
  • details of the Kickstart Scheme job placements and their location
  • supporting information to show that the job placements are new jobs and meet the Kickstart Scheme criteria
  • information about the support the organisation can give to develop employability skills of young people
